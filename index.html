Hello world! I'm a student in the Code Louisville program, in the September 2021 Front End Web Development (aka, FEWD) cohort. FEWD is a beginner level course.

The world of coding and web development is a challenging but exciting detour along my career journey. I have been an educator for most of my adult life, but this is not my first time attempting a career change. Back in the early 2000s I thought I'd be happier working in a field that allowed me to be creative. So I chased that dream for a few years, living my best life as a graphic design student by night and t-shirt designer by day (sometimes the other way around), until I ran out of money and had to drop out of art school a couple semesters shy of graduation. Building websites allows me to dig up some of those design skills from the archives of this old brain. It may take a minute, but I will get there . . .
